Help & Documentation

How to Log Workouts

Go to the "Workouts" page, enter your exercise type, sets, and reps, then click “Add Workout.”

How to Send a Challenge

Visit the "Friends" page, select a friend, and click “Send Challenge.”

Understanding Boss Battles

Earn points throughout the week to defeat the weekly boss. Check progress on the “Boss” page.

Badges and Streaks

Complete weekly goals and maintain streaks to unlock new badges.
