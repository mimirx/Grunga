1. Main Menu & Score Tracking

The Dashboard at a Glance

The main screen (Home) serves as your command center. It displays your personalized greeting and the following critical statistics:

  • Total Lifetime Points: Your cumulative score since joining Grunga.
  • Weekly Points: Your score for the current Monday-to-Sunday cycle, used for Boss Battles.
  • Daily Points: Your score for the current day, used for Streak qualification.
  • Streak Count: Your current consecutive days of meeting the daily workout milestone.

The Weekly Progress chart provides a visual track of your effort leading up to the Boss Battle, and the current Weekly Boss's artwork is prominently featured.

2. Weekly Points & The Reset Mechanic

The Role of Weekly Points

Points are earned primarily from logging workouts and secondarily from winning friend challenges. The weekly total acts as your contribution to defeating the weekly boss.

The Weekly Total resets every week at midnight (00:00) on Sunday night/Monday morning. This ensures you must stay consistent weekly and cannot carry points over.

3. Logging a Workout for Points

Data Input and Storage

Use the "Workouts" page to accurately record your activity. Required fields include:

  • Exercise Type: e.g., Running, Squats, Push-ups.
  • Duration/Reps: e.g., 30 minutes, 15 reps.
  • Sets/Reps: for strength training.

Clicking "Add Workout" calculates the points based on difficulty and updates your totals instantly.

The Streak Mechanic

5. Boss Battles and Rewards

Fighting the Boss

The Boss Battle is your weekly fitness mission. You fight the boss by earning Weekly Points.

  • HP Reduction: Boss HP decreases as you earn points.
  • Outcome: If you reach the weekly goal, you win and earn a badge. If not, you lose the week.

The Boss resets every Monday at midnight.

6. Badge Inventory and Unlock Details

Viewing Your Trophies

The "Badges" page displays all badges. Earned badges appear in color, while locked ones appear in grayscale.
